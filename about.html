About Hamro Bakery

Our Story

Hamro Bakery was founded in 2010 by passionate baker Rotimaya Shrestha. What started as a small home-based business quickly grew into a beloved local bakery, known for its high-quality ingredients and mouthwatering treats.

Our mission is to bring joy to our community through delicious, freshly baked goods made with love and care. We believe in using only the finest ingredients and traditional baking methods to create unforgettable flavors.
